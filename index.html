FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Do you bring your own water and equipment?

Yes. We arrive with everything needed for standard jobs.

How long does it take?

Most homes take 1–3 hours depending on window count and add-ons. Storefronts are usually 10–30 minutes.

Do you do same-day work?

Sometimes. If you’re flexible, you’ll have better odds.

What’s included in “detail work”?

Edges, sills wipe-down, light cobweb removal, and a final inspection for streaks/drips.
